About Luisa

Here you can find out some (hopefully) interesting facts about me and what I am to do with this blog.

Hello! I'm Luisa Esteban and currently I am a 4th year student at the University of Victoria, majoring in Anthropology and minoring and Art History and Visual Studies. Since I was a kid, I was always interestng in the arts and sciences so studying anthropolgy is a good balance between the two.

From Far and Wide

To begin, I was born in Winnepeg, MB, lived in Ottawa, ON, and currently live in Yellowknife, NT before coming to Victoria and at one point I did live in the Philippines. I come from a variety of places, each with their own qualities that made me call them home and if I were to pick a favourite, I would probably pick Victoria.

A question I would usually be asked (aside from "Is Yellowknife in the Yukon?") is "Why did you choose to come to Victoria?" and personally that is a very easy question.

So Why Did I Choose to Come Here?

I decided to come here because I loved being in BC and I heard that the program is pretty great. I knew that I didn't want to go to many other schools for my undergraduate degree and I also didn't want to go to a school where a lot of my high school friends were going (there are only a few at UVic) and it wasn't because they were bad friends, I wanted to be able to meet new people and experience most things on my own.

My Hopes for this Blog

I'm relatively new to the whole scope of blogging (in this format), using HTML & CSS, but being able to learn all these skills makes me feel a little more confident in knowing that I can apply these skills to possible careers one day. I hope to learn well and I know that I will be able to take away plenty of skills from this Portfolio Project.

There are projects here and there I hope to have completed soon and have up on the website. Thank you and stay tuned!
